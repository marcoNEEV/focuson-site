Privacy Policy – FocusON Flow

3. Third-Party Services

FocusON Flow does not integrate with third-party analytics, advertising networks, or social media SDKs.

4. Children’s Privacy

FocusON Flow is intended for general use and does not collect data from anyone, including children.
