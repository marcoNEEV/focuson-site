FocusON Flow Support

Welcome to the support page for FocusON Flow, the minimalist focus timer for macOS.

If you have any questions, feedback, or run into issues, we’re here to help.

Contact

Email: support@optimistai.org

Common topics

  • Questions about how the focus and break phases work
  • Issues with installing or running the app on your Mac
  • Bug reports or unexpected behavior
  • Suggestions for future improvements

What to include in your message

To help us answer faster, please include:

  • Your macOS version (e.g. macOS 15.x)
  • Your Mac model (e.g. MacBook Air M2)
  • The version of FocusON Flow you’re using
  • A short description of what happened, and if possible, steps to reproduce it

Privacy

FocusON Flow does not collect personal data or analytics from inside the app. For more details, please see our Privacy Policy.
